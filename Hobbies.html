Airsoft

It is a great mix of strategy, communication, and hands-on problem solving, and I value clear roles and teamwork to keep squads moving as one. I am part of a growing community of players called Naughty Dogz Airsoft, and I actively helped organize the team for large events such as Atlantic Front and Fulda Gap.
